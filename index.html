About

I am a high school student passionate about electronics, robotics, and hands on engineering projects. I enjoy turning concepts into functional prototypes by integrating CAD design, mechanical fabrication, circuit wiring, and embedded programming.

I will be attending the University of British Columbia in September 2026, entering the Engineering program with the goal of pursuing Mechatronics Engineering. My long term focus is on building intelligent electromechanical systems that combine mechanical design, control systems, and embedded hardware.

I have worked extensively with Arduino and Raspberry Pi platforms, developing projects that strengthen my understanding of sensor integration, motor control, mechanical structures, and system level reliability. Beyond my own projects, I support other students in a multi grade electronics class and actively participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition community.

What I do

  • Embedded programming

    Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects, sensors, motors, and basic control systems for robotics builds.

  • Electronics prototyping

    Breadboarding, basic circuit design, testing with a multimeter, and soldering on perfboard.

  • CAD and 3D printing

    Fusion 360 and OnShape for mechanical design, plus slicing and printing on Ender series printers.

  • Team leadership

    Leading build planning, delegating work, and supporting teammates with clear explanations and structure.
